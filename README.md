# Velvet & Vector

**AI Fashion House | Conceptual Project**

Velvet & Vector is a **futuristic AI-driven fashion concept** where garments are **programmable, responsive, and interactive**.  
This repository explores the intersection of **fashion design, wearable technology, and artificial intelligence**, imagining a future where clothing adapts to the wearer’s emotions and movements.

---

## Project Highlights

- **Programmable Fabrics:** Garments that change color, texture, or pattern in response to biometric signals such as heart rate or movement.  
- **Futuristic Aesthetics:** Avant-garde, fluid designs with iridescent textures, holographic effects, and glassmorphism-inspired visuals.  
- **Creative Exploration:** A conceptual playground for designers and developers to imagine the next generation of fashion technology.  
- **Interactive Design:** Emphasizes how clothing can become a personal, responsive experience rather than static apparel.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to **demonstrate a conceptual AI-driven fashion brand** while practicing **version control, project organization, and documentation**.  
It serves as a **creative portfolio piece** as well as an exploration of **how AI and wearable technology could influence future fashion**.
